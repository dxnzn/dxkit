<objective>
Fix two confirmed defects from the external PR #4 code review, with regression tests.

1. Stale mountDapp epilogue (src/shell.ts:422-432): after `await lifecycle.mount(...)`, the
   epilogue emits a catch-up dx:route:subpath and unconditionally writes `currentPath = freshPath`.
   A superseded/invalidated call's continuation runs that unguarded write (swallowing a later
   same-dapp sub-path nav), and for two same-dapp calls the `getCurrentDapp() === manifest.id`
   guard also passes for the stale call (spurious/duplicate subpath). Fix: `lifecycle.mount()`
   returns whether THIS call committed; `mountDapp` gates the entire epilogue on that flag.
2. normalizeRoute doesn't trim (src/shell.ts:252-260): ' /a' becomes '/ /a', '/a ' keeps its
   trailing space — both silently unreachable, violating D-06. Fix: trim at the top of
   normalization (whitespace-only already returns null).

Purpose: Keep last-navigation-wins (D-01) correct through the invalidate-then-renavigate flow
Phase 4 made reachable, and keep D-06 (reject-or-fix, never silent) honest for whitespace routes.
Output: Hardened src/lifecycle.ts + src/shell.ts, a deterministic stress regression test, and
normalizeRoute unit tests. `make test` fully green, lint clean.
</objective>
